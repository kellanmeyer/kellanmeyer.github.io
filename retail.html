Korean Skincare is rarely sold in stores, and the popular Amazon is often full of fake products. To help you guys, I've compiled a list of three websites that offer shipping to the US. Plus, all of these websites offer free shipping over a certain spending threshold!

Olive Young

Olive Young is the biggest retailer for beauty products in South Korea, with over a thousand stores throughout the country. While there aren't any stores in the US, they have a global website that ships to the US! Olive Young is the best for finding value boxes, new member perks, and discounted prices on double packs of products.

Visit Olive Young

YesStyle

YesStyle is an online retailer of Korean Skincare that ships globally. They are the best for finding flash sales, regular discounts on a majority of products, and free express shipping on big orders. It is important to note that their regular shipping is very slow (2-3 weeks).

Visit YesStyle

Style Korean

Style Korean is another online retailer of Korean Skincare that ships globally. Like YesStyle, they are also good for flash sales and regular discounts on many products. However, I found that their shipping is much faster than YesStyle!

Visit Style Korean
